A therapeutic hands-on creative gathering

"CERAMICANDO STYLE: STAMPING CLAY WITH CREATIVITY"

An in-person experience led by Cris Attico, designed for those who seek to slow down, make new friends, spark creativity, and create with their own hands.

More than a ceramics class, this is an invitation to a mindful break from routine. A moment of presence, connection, and creative expression through clay, stamps, and textures.

Here, it doesn't matter if you've never worked with ceramics before: the focus is on the process, the sensory experience, and the exchange that happens when we touch clay and come together with others who share the same energy and intention.

What you'll experience

During the session, you will:

• Create your own clay piece even if you've never taken a class
• Learn stamp application techniques
• Explore texture composition
• Stimulate focus and creativity in a welcoming environment
• Breathe, relax, and pause the hectic pace of everyday life

Each participant creates their own piece. Finishing and glazing will be handled by the studio. Pieces can be picked up 45 days after the session.

Who this workshop is for

This gathering is ideal for:

• People with or without ceramics experience
• Those looking for a break from routine
• Anyone interested in handcraft and well-being
• Those who want to stimulate creativity in a practical way
• Adults who want to enjoy an artistic and relaxing experience

No prior knowledge required.

What's included

• All materials used during the workshop
• Guidance throughout the entire activity
• Stamp application and composition techniques
• Firing and glazing of your piece
• Your finished piece ready to take home

You don't need to bring anything: just show up and create.
