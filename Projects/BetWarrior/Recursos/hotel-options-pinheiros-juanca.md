## Hotel Notes

### Quality Hotel Faria Lima ★★★★
**Address:** Rua Diogo Moreira, 247 — Pinheiros
Best price-to-proximity ratio on this list. Features a pool, gym, and sauna — a plus for multi-day stays. Faria Lima Metro Station is a 6-minute walk away. Recommended as first choice.

### ibis Styles SP Faria Lima ★★★
**Address:** Rua Tavares Cabral, 61 — Pinheiros
Most affordable option and closest to the office. Comfortable for short stays, no frills. Ideal if the visit is only 1–2 nights.

### Address SP Faria Lima by Intercity ★★★★
**Address:** Av. Faria Lima area — Pinheiros
Modern Brazilian chain with a business/tech focus. Well-equipped rooms, good connectivity, and a contemporary design. Good mid-range option.

### Radisson Pinheiros ★★★★
**Address:** Rua Cristiano Viana, 318 — Pinheiros
Most premium 4-star option on this list. Breakfast included. Good restaurant on-site. Best choice if comfort and facilities are a priority.

### Mercure São Paulo Pinheiros ★★★★
**Address:** Rua Capote Valente, 500 — Jardim Paulista
Reliable Accor brand, consistently well-rated. Slightly farther from the office but fully walkable in good weather. Solid all-round option.

## Important Note on Travel Times
Car estimates are for outside peak hours. The Pinheiros / Faria Lima corridor has heavy traffic between **5–8 PM**. Walking or using a shared scooter (Lime/Yellow) is often faster than a car for short distances during rush hour.
